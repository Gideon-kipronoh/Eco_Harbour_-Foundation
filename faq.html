Donation

Eco_Harbor is committed to empowering communities to become stewards of their environment through education, capacity-building workshops, and grassroots advocacy. By collaborating with local partners, governments, and international organizations, Eco_Harbor works to create lasting positive change for both people and the planet..

Frequently Asked Questions

Eco_Harbor Foundation is dedicated to providing assistance and hope to children through our sponsorship programs. By becoming a sponsor, you can make a meaningful difference in the lives of vulnerable children, providing them with essential support and opportunities for a better future.

  • In which state do you work most in Kenya?

    Eco_Harbor Foundation operates extensively in several counties across Kenya, focusing on regions where environmental conservation and community development are paramount. Some of the counties where we have significant presence and active projects include Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, and Machakos, among others. Our initiatives are strategically designed to address the unique environmental challenges and socio-economic needs of each region.

  • How to become a volunteer in Kenya?

    If you're interested in volunteering with Eco_Harbor Foundation in Kenya, you can start by exploring our volunteer opportunities on our website or reaching out to our local offices in Kenya. We welcome individuals with various skills, backgrounds, and interests to join us in our efforts to make a positive impact on the environment and communities in Kenya. Whether you're passionate about conservation, education, or community development, there are diverse roles available for volunteers to contribute their time and expertise.

  • How to donate on our site using our form?

    Donating on our site is easy and secure. Simply visit our donation page and fill out the donation form with your details, including the amount you wish to donate and any specific instructions or designations for your donation. You can choose to make a one-time donation or set up a recurring donation schedule. Once you submit the form, you'll be guided through the payment process to complete your donation securely. Thank you for your generosity and support!

  • What and where are the headquarters of Eco-Harbor?

    Eco-Harbor Foundation's headquarters are located in Nairobi, Kenya. From our headquarters, we coordinate our global initiatives and operations, working closely with local offices, partners, and volunteers worldwide to advance our mission of environmental conservation and sustainable development. Our Nairobi headquarters serve as a hub for strategic planning, program management, and collaboration with stakeholders to address pressing environmental challenges and promote positive change.
